## ── GREETINGS & FAREWELLS ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
yaar / bhai / dost / buddy          = friend | address term for close person
kya baat hai / kya scene hai        = what's up / what's happening
kya chal raha / kya ho raha         = what's going on
hello bhai / hi yaar                = casual hello
bye bhai / chalte hain / chalo      = goodbye / let's go
phir milenge / phir baat karte hain = see you again / talk later
subah ki shuruwaat / good morning yaar = good morning (casual)
raat ko milte hain = see you at night / good night (casual)


## ── AGREEMENT & CONFIRMATION ──────────────────────────────────────────────
